### Career growth using AI

The goal is all four of these, but they compound in a specific order — don't chase them all at once (spreading thin is a known failure mode):

1. **Charge more via AI speed** — use AI agents/workflows to deliver dev + SEO work faster and better, justifying higher rates on the same services. _This directly serves the $3k target._
2. **The December capability: an Agentic SEO system** — an AI agent workflow that runs audits, keyword research, and content briefs semi-automatically. This is the ONE skill bet for 2026: it makes lever #1 real and becomes the engine behind SEO retainers.
3. **Positioning / branding** — become known as the "AI-powered dev & SEO": content, portfolio, case studies. Builds naturally on #1 and #2 once there are results to show (Eastern Exteriors is the first case study).
4. **Build AI products** — the long game: turn the agentic SEO system into something productized/sellable. Don't start here; earn it from #2.

_→ The dedicated Chess Moves session on this should turn #2 into a concrete build plan with milestones._

### Personal (faith, family, health)

- **Faith: start each workday with God.** Prayer or reading before opening the laptop — ties "God first" directly to the work rhythm, every single day.
- **Family: one protected family day per week.** A full day with zero work — no laptop, no client messages.
- **Health: exercise rhythm + sleep discipline.** A weekly exercise rhythm and a consistent sleep schedule — sleep especially, since stalling-when-overwhelmed is the known pattern and bad sleep feeds it.
